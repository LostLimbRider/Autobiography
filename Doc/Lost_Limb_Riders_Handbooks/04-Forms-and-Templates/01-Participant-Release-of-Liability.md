# Assumption of Risk

I acknowledge that participation in Lost Limb Riders activities may involve inherent risks, including but not limited to:

* Motorcycle riding and transportation
* Group rides
* Meetings
* Community service projects
* Volunteer activities
* Fundraising events
* Outdoor activities
* Recreational activities
* Physical activities
* Travel to and from events
* Interaction with other participants
* Weather-related hazards
* Road hazards
* Equipment failure
* Slips, trips, and falls
* Injuries caused by other participants or third parties

I understand that these risks may result in serious injury, permanent disability, illness, property damage, or death.

I voluntarily choose to participate despite these risks.

---

# Participant Responsibilities

I agree to:

* Follow all applicable laws.
* Follow all event rules and safety instructions.
* Operate any vehicle in a safe and legal manner.
* Wear required safety equipment.
* Immediately report unsafe conditions.
* Exercise reasonable care for my own safety.
* Conduct myself respectfully toward others.
* Refrain from participating if I am impaired by alcohol, drugs, medication, or illness in a way that would make participation unsafe.

---

# Release of Liability

To the fullest extent permitted by applicable law, I release and hold harmless Lost Limb Riders, Inc., its officers, directors, employees, volunteers, members, sponsors, event organizers, agents, and partnering organizations from claims arising out of my participation in organization activities, **except to the extent prohibited by law**.

This release includes claims relating to:

* Personal injury
* Property damage
* Medical expenses
* Lost wages
* Disability
* Emotional distress
* Wrongful death
* Other losses resulting from participation

This release does **not** waive claims that cannot legally be waived under applicable law.

# Medical Treatment Authorization

I understand I am financially responsible for any medical expenses incurred.
