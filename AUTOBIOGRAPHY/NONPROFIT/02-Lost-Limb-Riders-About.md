# About the Lost Limb Riders

## Mission

To provide peer support, community, and hope to amputees and their families through shared experiences, group activities, and mentorship.

## Vision

A world where no amputee feels alone in their journey.

---

## Who We Serve

- Recent amputees (traumatic, medical, or congenital)
- Long-term amputees seeking community
- Families and caregivers of amputees
- Veterans and first responders with limb loss
- Children with limb differences

---

## Core Programs

### 1. Peer Visits
Trained amputee volunteers visit new amputees in hospitals and rehab centers to offer support, answer questions, and show what's possible.

### 2. Group Rides
Regular motorcycle rides that build community, create fun, and remind amputees that they can still do the things they love.

### 3. Mentorship Program
One-on-one matching between new and experienced amputees for ongoing support.

### 4. Prosthetic Assistance Fund
Financial help for amputees who need prosthetic components, repairs, or upgrades not covered by insurance.

### 5. Community Events
BBQs, fundraisers, awareness events, and social gatherings.
