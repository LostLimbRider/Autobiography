# Keynote Template — Youth / Schools
## Overcoming Obstacles
### By John Thompson

**Audience:** High schools, youth groups, sports teams, at-risk youth programs
**Tone:** Relatable, energetic, uses humor, direct
**Runtime:** 15–25 minutes (adjust by trimming sections)

---

### Core Angle

Connect with young people where they are. Use humor (the stump joke, Roxy stories). Talk about real struggles without overwhelming them. Focus on the message that hard times don't define you — your choices do.

---

### I. Opening (2 min)

"So I lost my leg. You know what the first thing I thought was when I saw it? 'That looks like a giant penis.'"

(Wait for reaction.)

"I'm serious. Nobody tells you that. So now you know. Life is weird. Deal with it."

---

### II. Bad Things Happen (5 min)

- Bad things happen to everyone
- It's not your fault, but it is your responsibility
- You can't control what happens; you can control what you do next

---

### III. My Bad Things (5 min)

Quick versions of illness, addiction, loss, heart attack, cancer, amputation.

"I'm not telling you this to depress you. I'm telling you because if I can survive all that, you can survive whatever high school throws at you."

---

### IV. What Saved Me (5 min)

- Family
- Faith
- Humor
- Roxy
- Not quitting

"The people who love you — they matter more than you think. And if you don't have people who love you, find a dog. Dogs don't judge."

---

### V. Closing (2 min)

"You're tougher than you know. Life is going to hit you. Maybe it already has. But you can get back up. I promise you. Just don't quit."

"I Can. I Will."

"So can you."
