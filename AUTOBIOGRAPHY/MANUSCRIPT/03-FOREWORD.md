# Foreword

My name is John Thompson.

I've been knocked down more times than I can count.

I've survived serious illness, addiction, the loss of loved ones, a heart attack, cancer, and the loss of my right leg below the knee.

Each time, I had a choice.

I could quit.

Or I could get back up.

This book is the story of choosing to get back up.

It isn't about feeling sorry for myself. It's about faith, family, determination, and finding purpose after life's toughest challenges.

My hope is that if you're facing your own battle, you'll find encouragement in these pages.

No matter what life throws at you...

I Can. I Will.
