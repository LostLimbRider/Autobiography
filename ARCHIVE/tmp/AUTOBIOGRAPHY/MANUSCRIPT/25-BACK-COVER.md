# Back Cover Description

John Thompson has been knocked down more times than most people can imagine.

Serious illness. Addiction. The loss of loved ones. A heart attack. Cancer. The amputation of his right leg below the knee.

Each time, he had a choice: quit or get back up.

He got back up.

I CAN. I WILL. is the true story of one man's refusal to stay down. It's raw, honest, and at times unexpectedly funny. From hospital beds to motorcycle rides, from rock bottom to recovery, John's journey proves that the human spirit is tougher than any challenge life can throw at it.

If you're in the middle of your own battle, this book is for you.

You can get through this.

I Can. I Will.
