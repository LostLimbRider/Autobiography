# Afterword

I wrote this book for one reason: because I promised myself that if I made it through, I'd tell the story.

Not for attention. Not for money. For the person who needs to hear it.

If you're that person, I want you to know something. You're not alone. You're not broken. You're not finished.

Whatever you're going through right now, you can get through it. I'm not saying it'll be easy. It probably won't be. But you can do it.

Take it from a guy who lost his leg, his health, his peace of mind, and damn near his life.

I Can. I Will.

So can you.

Now go prove it.
