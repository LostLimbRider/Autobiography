## Form Use Guidelines

### For Participants
- **Program Enrollment Form** — Complete at first participation
- **Emergency Contact Form** — Required for all participants
- **Liability Release** — Required before any activity
- **Photo Release** — Complete before photos/videos
- **Accessibility Request** — Submit if accommodations needed
- **Feedback Form** — Complete after each program (optional)

### For Volunteers
- **Volunteer Application** — Submit to apply
- **Emergency Contact Form** — Required before first shift
- **Liability Release** — Required before any activity
- **Photo Release** — Complete if appearing in materials
- **Hours Tracking Form** — Submit monthly
- **Feedback Form** — Complete after activities

### For Staff
- **All forms above** — Maintain in participant/volunteer files
- **Incident Report** — Complete within 24 hours of any incident
- **Accessibility Accommodations** — Process and document all requests
- **Hours Tracking** — Review and verify monthly

---

## Document Maintenance & Updates

All forms should be reviewed annually and updated as:
- Legal requirements change
- Program policies evolve
- New safety procedures are established
- Feedback indicates improvements needed

**Responsibility:** Executive Director and Program Directors

**Approval Process:** Form changes require Program Director approval. Legal/policy changes require Executive Director and Board review.

---

## Storage & Confidentiality

- **Original forms** stored in secure filing system (paper or encrypted digital)
- **Participant files** accessible only to authorized staff
- **Medical/accessibility information** stored separately with additional security
- **Incident reports** stored with insurance documentation
- **Volunteer files** stored in HR/Volunteer Coordinator office
- **Backup copies** maintained digitally in encrypted format
