This directory contains operational manuals for all active Lost Limb Riders programs. Each manual documents program objectives, procedures, volunteer guidelines, participant tracking, safety protocols, and quality standards.

## How to Use These Manuals

- **For Program Coordinators:** Use the full manual as your operational guide
- **For Volunteers:** Review relevant sections for your role and refer to volunteer handbooks
- **For Staff:** Use for training, onboarding, and quality assurance
- **For Updates:** Report needed changes to the Program Director or Executive Director

All manuals should be reviewed and updated annually or when procedures change.
