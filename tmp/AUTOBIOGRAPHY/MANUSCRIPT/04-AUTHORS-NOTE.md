# Author's Note

I sat down to write this book more than once. Every time, I talked myself out of it.

Who wants to read about my struggles? Who cares what I went through?

Then I realized something. When I was in the hospital bed, when I was staring at the ceiling wondering if I'd ever walk again, when I was at my lowest — I would have given anything to hear from someone who made it through.

Not a superhero. Just a regular person who refused to quit.

That's what this book is. One regular person's story. No heroics. No pretending I had it all figured out. Just the truth about what happened, how I got through it, and the people who helped me along the way.

The names are real. The events are real. The emotions are real.

Some of the language is rough. I don't apologize for that. When you've had your life turned upside down enough times, you stop caring about being polite. You start caring about being honest.

This is my honest story.

Read it however you want. Take what helps. Leave the rest.

But if you take one thing, let it be this: you can get back up.

I did it. So can you.

— John Thompson
