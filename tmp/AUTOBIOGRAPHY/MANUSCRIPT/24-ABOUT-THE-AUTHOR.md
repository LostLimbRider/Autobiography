# About the Author

John Thompson is a husband, father, amputee, cancer survivor, heart attack survivor, recovering addict, motorcycle rider, and motivational speaker.

He has survived what should have killed him — multiple times. Instead of staying down, he chose to get back up, and now he travels to share his story with anyone who needs to hear it.

John lives with his wife, Genie, and his dog, Roxy. He rides with the Lost Limb Riders. He believes in faith, family, and refusing to quit.

He is living proof that you can lose almost everything and still build a life worth living.

I Can. I Will. That's not just a motto. That's how he lives.
