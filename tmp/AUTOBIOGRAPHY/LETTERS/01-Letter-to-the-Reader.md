# A Letter to the Reader

Dear Friend,

If you're holding this book, you're probably going through something. Or you know someone who is. Or you're just curious about how a guy who lost his leg, his health, and damn near his sanity is still standing.

I'll tell you the truth: it wasn't easy. But it was worth it.

Every time I got knocked down, I learned something. Every time I thought I couldn't get back up, I found a reason to try anyway. Those reasons were my wife, my daughters, my dog, my faith, and a community of people who refused to let me quit.

I wrote this book because I promised myself that if I made it through, I'd share what I learned. Not to impress anyone. Not to get attention. Because someone out there needs to hear that they can make it too.

Maybe that someone is you.

If it is, here's what I want you to know: you're stronger than you think. You have more fight in you than you know. And no matter how bad it gets, you can get back up.

I did it. You can too.

Keep going.

— John Thompson

I Can. I Will.
