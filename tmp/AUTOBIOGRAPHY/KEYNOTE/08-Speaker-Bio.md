# Speaker Bio — John Thompson

## Short Bio (50 words)

John Thompson is a husband, father, amputee, cancer survivor, and motivational speaker. After surviving illness, addiction, a heart attack, cancer, and the loss of his leg, he now shares his story of resilience and determination with audiences across the country. He is living proof that you can get back up.

## Medium Bio (150 words)

John Thompson has been knocked down more times than most people can imagine.

He has survived serious illness, battled addiction, lost loved ones, suffered a heart attack, beaten cancer, and undergone a below-the-knee amputation. Each time, he chose to get back up.

Today, John is a motivational speaker who shares his story with corporations, hospitals, recovery centers, schools, and nonprofit organizations. His message — "I Can. I Will." — is a powerful reminder that resilience is a choice, not a gift.

John speaks with honesty, humor, and heart. He doesn't pretend to have all the answers, but he knows one thing for certain: quitting is permanent, and everything else is temporary.

He lives with his wife, Genie, and his dog, Roxy. He rides with the Lost Limb Riders. He believes in faith, family, and refusing to stay down.

## Long Bio (300 words)

John Thompson has survived what should have killed him. Multiple times.

Serious illness nearly took him out early. Addiction nearly destroyed him from the inside. The loss of loved ones broke his heart long before his heart attack did. Then came cancer, followed by the amputation of his right leg below the knee.

Most people would have stopped after the first one. John kept going.

Born and raised with the belief that you earn what you get, John carried that work ethic through every challenge life threw at him. When his health failed, he fought back. When addiction took hold, he climbed out. When his heart gave out, he rebuilt. When cancer came, he beat it. When his leg was taken, he learned to walk again.

Today, John shares his story from stages across the country. He tailors each speech to his audience — whether he's inspiring physical therapy patients to push through the pain, speaking to recovering addicts about the climb back to sobriety, teaching corporate leaders about resilience, or making teenagers laugh with jokes about his prosthetic and stories about his dog, Roxy.

His message is simple but powerful: "I Can. I Will."

Not because he's special. Because he refused to quit.

John lives with his wife, Genie, and his dog, Roxy. He is an active member of the Lost Limb Riders, a community of amputees who support each other through riding, laughter, and brotherhood. He credits his survival to faith, family, and the stubborn refusal to stay down.

Whether you need a keynote speaker for a corporate event, a fundraiser, a recovery center, a school, or a conference, John Thompson delivers a message your audience will remember long after they leave the room.

Book John. He'll show up. He'll deliver. He'll prove that no matter what life throws at you — you can get back up.

I Can. I Will.
