# Short Keynote — 20 Minutes
## I Can. I Will.
### By John Thompson

**Audience:** General — shorter event slots, breakout sessions, smaller gatherings
**Tone:** Inspirational, direct, impactful
**Runtime:** 18–22 minutes

---

### I. Opening (2 min)

"I lost my leg below the knee. Before that, I survived cancer, a heart attack, addiction, and more. I'm not telling you this to impress you. I'm telling you because if I can make it through all of that, you can make it through whatever you're facing."

---

### II. The Hits (6 min)

Speed through the major challenges:

- Illness
- Addiction — "I hit bottom. I mean that literally."
- Loss
- Heart attack
- Cancer
- Amputation

One to two minutes each. Focus on the feeling of each moment, not the details.

---

### III. The Turning Point (4 min)

"The moment I decided I wasn't going to quit. Not because I was brave. Because quitting was permanent and I wasn't done yet."

---

### IV. The Tools That Saved Him (4 min)

- Family (Genie, daughters)
- Faith
- Community (Lost Limb Riders)
- Humor ("my stump looks like a giant penis")

---

### V. I Can. I Will. (3 min)

"Two sentences. Four words. They saved my life. I said them every morning. Some days I believed it. Some days I didn't. I said it anyway."

---

### VI. Closing (1 min)

"You have more strength than you know. You just haven't needed it yet. But when you do, it'll be there. I promise."

"I Can. I Will."

"Thank you."
